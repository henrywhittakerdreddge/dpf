The Data Protection Foundation CIC (the DPF) is a nonpartisan, not-for-profit, multidisciplinary research organisation dedicated to investigating legal issues surrounding infringements of data protection regulation.

The DPF aims to bring together legal academics, barristers, technologists, policymakers, regulators, and social scientists to consider a range of important but currently unresolved jurisprudential questions surrounding corporate use and misuse of personal data. It will publish this jurisprudential research in open-access channels.

Please get in touch if you’d like to participate or learn more about us.
